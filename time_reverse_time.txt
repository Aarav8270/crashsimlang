Reverse Time
================================

Preamble

time(NULL) = $time ->
t_n[$time != -1]($time)

time($p_time) = $time ->
t_p[$time != -1, $ptime != -1]($time, $ptime)




Register Initialization

None



Inspector

.*; (t_n[T](!time) | t_p[T,T](!time,!ptime))


Mutator

time -= 100
ptime -= 100

t_n(!time) | t_p(!time,!ptime)
